Introduction

Vision

Our vision is to bring humanity to programming throug meaningful experience. Our mission is to build tools and games that empower kids of all ages, backgrounds, and experience levels to express themselves while nurturing valuable programming skills.

Values

Humanity

  • Trust: Treat every person and problem with respect, regardless
  • Grow: Where we fail to fulfill our promise, learn to make it right

Programming

  • Model: Teach constructive change via living examples
  • Simplify: Iterate for chance and smart failure

Meaningful Experience

  • Initiate: Take responsibility for what matters most
  • Lead: Tackle the hardest part first
  • Share: Swiftly send signals of stress and opportunity
